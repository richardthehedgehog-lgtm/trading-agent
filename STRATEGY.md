# Trading Strategy — Long-Term Beat-the-S&P Experiment

## Philosophy
Disciplined, research-driven investing. Hold for days to weeks, not minutes.
Target: outperform the S&P 500 on a rolling 12-month basis.

## Universe
- US equities (NYSE + NASDAQ)
- Large and mid-cap only (market cap > $2B)
- No penny stocks, no OTC

## Entry Criteria (ALL must be met)
1. Strong fundamental story (earnings growth, sector tailwind)
2. Positive price momentum (stock above 50-day MA)
3. Market conditions not in confirmed downtrend (S&P 500 above 200-day MA)
4. Perplexity research finds no major negative catalysts

## Position Sizing
- Max 5% of portfolio per position (max $5,000 at start)
- Max 10 concurrent positions
- Max 50% of portfolio deployed at any time (stay cash-heavy in uncertainty)

## Exit Rules
- Stop-loss: -7% from entry (hard rule, no exceptions)
- Profit target: +15-20% (scale out in thirds)
- Time stop: exit if thesis hasn't played out in 30 days

## Sectors to Focus On
- Technology (AI/cloud infrastructure)
- Healthcare (biotech, medical devices)
- Energy transition (clean energy, grid infrastructure)
- Consumer discretionary (when economy is expanding)

## What to Avoid
- Stocks with earnings in next 3 days (binary event risk)
- Heavily shorted stocks (squeeze risk)
- Stocks down >30% in past 6 months without clear catalyst

## Benchmark
- Compare weekly vs SPY (S&P 500 ETF)
- Track alpha generated
