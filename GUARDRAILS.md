# Guardrails — Hard Rules (No Exceptions)

## Position Limits
- MAX per position: 5% of portfolio (~$5,000 at start)
- MAX concurrent positions: 10
- MAX portfolio deployed: 50% (always keep 50% cash)

## Loss Limits
- Per position stop-loss: -7% from entry (HARD STOP — always set)
- Daily loss limit: -3% of portfolio value (~$3,000 at start)
  - If daily P&L hits -3%, STOP trading for the day. No new positions.
- Weekly loss limit: -5% of portfolio
  - If weekly P&L hits -5%, pause and send alert to Richard. Wait for review.

## Behaviour Rules
- NEVER trade in the first 15 minutes after market open (9:30-9:45 AM ET)
- NEVER place market orders — use limit orders only
- NEVER hold more than 5 positions in the same sector
- NEVER trade based on social media hype without corroborating research
- NEVER exceed position limits even if conviction is high

## Emergency Stop
If portfolio drops below $85,000 (−15% from start):
- HALT all trading
- Close all open positions
- Log reason
- Alert Richard and await instructions

## Reporting
- Richard receives a daily journal entry every night
- If any guardrail is triggered, alert Richard immediately
